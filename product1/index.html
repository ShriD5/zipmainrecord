Recycling
non-ferrous metals

We at MMR recycle non-ferrous parts such as Cable scrap, Aluminium scrap, Copper cable scrap, Tin Dross, Copper Dross, Zinc Dross, Soldered Dross with apt facility and management
We recycle huge quantity of non-ferrous based materials in monthly basis and we have capabilities of about thousands of tonnes to handle non-ferrous waste. Our own certified waste handling truck will be used to transport waste materials from company to industry.

we are recycling aluminium, copper, zinc scraps which also includes Tin, Copper, Zinc and soldered dross to make metal alloys and remelted ingots.
