Who are we?

CORPORATE VIDEO

MMR recycling-Moogambigai metal refineries Is a waste management company. It is a part of JC groups of company which began its early operations in field of recycling in the year 1970. We are a group are specialised In handling of various scraps, mainly – e-waste processing, plastic waste, processing nonferrous metal processing and battery waste processing.

We have a total of 800+ employees working all over our group in 10 well built facilities located in Karnataka and Tamil Nadu Each of these facilities are well equipped with advanced automated and semi automated machineries and infrastructure in order to cater our handling needs
